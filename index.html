Death Doula

Looking for peace and dignity throughtout your terminal illness and/or end of life journey?

I am a Palliative Care nurse and Death Doula and am available to provide compassionate support for you and your loved ones throughout your unique journey.

Elegant Grace End of Life Doulas is a registered business with a sliding scale free rate to make end of life care affordable to you and your loved ones.

Services

    Services include but are not limited to:
  • Legacy work life review
  • Funeral Planning
  • Advanced Care Planning and Goals of Care
  • Guidance through all of your unique choices
  • End of Life care and support
  • Illness journey support
  • Grief and bereavement support
  • Spiritual support
  • Death and vigil planning
